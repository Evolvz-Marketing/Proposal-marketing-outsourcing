01The Approach

Rationale for an Outsourced Model

Running this properly is essentially two full-time roles worth of work - social, content, paid media, email, strategy, and reporting, all moving at once. Rather than hire and manage that directly, we bring in specialists who already do each discipline professionally, and keep tight oversight ourselves.

Speed to Specialist Quality

Each function - paid media, content, social - goes to a party that already does this daily for other brands, instead of being learned from scratch in-house.

One Point of Accountability

Strategy, agency management, and reporting stay in-house. Every outsourced party answers to a single person tracking whether it's actually working.

Decisions on Data, Not Guesses

By month four, every channel has run a full cycle. That's when we know - with real numbers - whether to scale, adjust, or stop.

Why This Period Matters

By the end of month four, the model will be validated on real performance data.

Each outsourcing partner will have completed a full operating cycle by that point. Performance data on sales, subscriber retention, and acquisition cost will determine whether the model is scaled, adjusted, or paused - based on evidence rather than assumptions. Only once the model is validated does it make sense to invest in automation - at that point, the focus shifts to automating the proven workflows in order to reduce the overall fixed costs.

05Beyond Month 4 - If We Continue

The actual scope - and the resulting effect on the fixed monthly costs - will be defined together once we reach that point, based on what the validation period shows is working.
